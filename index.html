How it works

Four steps from "rent is due" to "paid in full"

1. Check the balance

Tenants open their dashboard and see exactly what's owed — no calls to the office, no waiting for a statement.

2. Pay with STK Push

One tap sends a prompt straight to the tenant's phone. They confirm with their PIN — no paybill numbers to type or get wrong.

3. Get a digital receipt

The moment the API confirms payment, a receipt is generated and the balance updates — for both the tenant and the manager.
